All about me!

Hi, thanks for visiting my site. As you can probably tell my name is Chris Dickson. I am a current third year student at Napier University working on my Bachelors degree in web design and development. Originally a fully qualified chef of over 16 years, I decided that this career was no longer for me and made the choice to pursue a career in digital design and development which moved into web development as the years went on. Over the years between college and university I have developed a wide array of skills and learned technologies I would never have had the opportunity to use if I was still a chef.
I have over the years designed and developed websites for assignments but have done so using different technologies such as HTML/CSS, bootstrap, JavaScript, PHP/SQL, Python including Flask, PostgreSql.
I also have stong skills with Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, After Effects, Premier Pro as well as some 3d rendering and animation skills!
